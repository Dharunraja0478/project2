Vocabulary

"Vocabulary" refers to the set of words known and used by an individual or group of people. It encompasses all the words a person understands, recognizes, and can use in their language. Building a strong vocabulary is essential for effective communication, comprehension, and expression in any language. It involves learning the meanings, pronunciations, and usage of words in various contexts. Vocabulary can range from basic words used in everyday communication to specialized terminology used in specific fields or disciplines.
